Instructions:



To play, gather 2-4 people and pick a game piece. A calculator is not required (or banned) but makes life easier. You will need a stopwatch or use a phone to time 15 seconds.

Put said pieces on start and have the youngest in the group playing roll the two dice. Add the dice value rolled together then subtract two. Multiply the total to 180 then pick up a factor card. The equation looks like this: ((x+y-2)180)-factor card number. If the final total is 700 or more move to the Yellow Helix and pick a chance card.

Everyone would get 15 seconds If you are in the Yellow Helix for 3 turns it is game over for you. If there are more than 2 people the game carries on. If the total is under 700 move 1 space in the Red Helix. If you are in the Yellow Helix or the Red Helix, you always move 1 space.
To win the game, whoever gets to the finish first wins!

The Golden Helix
